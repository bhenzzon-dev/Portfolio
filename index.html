ABOUT ME

I am Jhon Bhenzzon Saure, a 22-year-old Full-Stack Developer, born on December 14, 2002.
I graduated from Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Muntinlupa with two diplomas:
an Associate in Computer Technology and a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

During my college years, I worked as a student employee and developed skills in photography and photo editing.
To support my studies, I also took on project-based website commissions, gaining early experience in web development.

During my on-the-job training (OJT) at Food Link Corps, I was tasked with creating a Food Ordering System as the sole IT personnel.
Alongside building the system, I also handled hardware, networking, and graphic design for the products.

My performance led the company to absorb me as a Junior Full-Stack Developer even before my OJT ended.
In the first months as a regular employee, I developed a Web-Based Operating System for the company,
which I continue to maintain, enhancing the interface, fixing hidden bugs, and managing the database.

These experiences strengthened my problem-solving, multitasking, graphic design, and full-stack development skills, fueling my passion
for building impactful, user-friendly web solutions.

I am highly motivated to grow as a developer,
continuously expanding my expertise in modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and Bootstrap,
while delivering reliable, end-to-end solutions.

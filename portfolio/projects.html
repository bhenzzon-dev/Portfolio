GNL Operating System (GNL OS) – Internal Operations Management System
Overview:
The GNL Operating System (GNL OS) is a web-based system developed to streamline and automate GNL’s internal operations. It centralizes multiple workflows, improving efficiency, accountability, and communication within the organization.
Key Features:
  • Material Requests: Engineers can submit material requests, which are routed to the office interface. Staff select suppliers, create Purchase Orders (POs), and forward them to the admin for approval and material release.

  • User-Friendly Interface: Clear dashboards for engineers, staff, and admins to manage their respective tasks easily.

  • Frameworks / Libraries: Bootstrap
